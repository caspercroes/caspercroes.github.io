About

Casper Croes is a solo independent game developer based in Flanders, Belgium.

Games

Alisa is a classic 90s style horror-themed game set in a fantasy universe inspired on the 1920s. You play as an Elite Royal Agent called Alisa. While she is chasing a wanted criminal, she ends up in an old victorian mansion. She tries to find a way out while being haunted by materialized/mechanized doll-like humanoids. Can you survive the Dollhouse?
